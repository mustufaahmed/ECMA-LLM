# Refund Policy

## Refund Processing Time
Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we initiate a refund to your original payment method. Refunds typically post within **3 to 5 business days** depending on your bank or card issuer.

## Refund Methods
- Credit / debit card purchases are refunded to the same card.
- Wallet or store-credit purchases are refunded to the same wallet.
- Gift-card purchases are refunded as ShopMax store credit.

## Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be granted for:
- Items returned after the 30-day window in exceptional cases
- Items returned with signs of use, missing parts, or damage not caused by us

## Duplicate Charges
If you see two identical charges for the same order, it is typically a pending pre-authorisation that will drop off within 5 business days. If both charges settle, contact support with your order number and we will issue a refund referenced as `REF-YYYY-XXXX` within 3-5 business days.

## Non-Refundable Fees
Original shipping charges on non-defective returns are non-refundable.
