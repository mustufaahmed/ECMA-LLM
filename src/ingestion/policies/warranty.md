# Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty
Electronics and appliances sold by ShopMax include a **90-day limited warranty** from the date of delivery. Defective items within the warranty period are eligible for repair, replacement, or refund.

## Coverage
The warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failure under normal use
- Missing parts or incorrect items shipped

## Not Covered
- Accidental damage, liquid damage, or physical abuse
- Damage from unauthorised repair
- Normal wear and tear (battery degradation under 20% after a year is considered normal)

## Filing a Warranty Claim
Contact support with your order number, a short description of the issue, and a photo if relevant. We will either ship a replacement or send a pre-paid return label for repair within 2 business days.
