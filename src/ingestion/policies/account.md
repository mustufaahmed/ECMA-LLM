# Account Policy

## Locked Accounts
Accounts are locked automatically after 5 failed sign-in attempts to protect your data. You will receive a password-reset email at the address on file.

## Pending Orders During Lock
Pending or in-progress orders are **NOT cancelled** while an account is locked. Order status continues to update normally and delivery is not affected. You will regain visibility to those orders as soon as the account is unlocked.

## Unlocking Your Account
1. Open the password-reset email from ShopMax (check spam).
2. Click the link (valid for 30 minutes).
3. Set a new password.

If the email does not arrive within 10 minutes, contact support with your registered email address.
