# Shipping Policy

## Delivery Times
- **Standard shipping:** 3-5 business days (free over $50)
- **Express shipping:** 1-2 business days (flat $12.99)
- **Same-day delivery:** available in select metro areas for an extra fee at checkout

## Tracking
Tracking numbers are emailed once the order ships. You can also find live tracking in your account under "My Orders".

## Carriers
ShopMax uses DHL, FedEx, and UPS depending on destination and weight. The carrier is shown on the order detail page.

## Failed Delivery
If a carrier cannot deliver after three attempts, the package is returned to our warehouse and refunded minus the original shipping fee.
