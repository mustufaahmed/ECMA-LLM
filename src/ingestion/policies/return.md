# Return Policy

## Return Window
Most items purchased from ShopMax can be returned within **30 days of delivery**. The 30-day window starts from the date the carrier marks the order as delivered, not the order date.

## Eligibility
To qualify for a return, the item must be:
- Unused and in the same condition you received it
- In its original packaging with all tags and accessories
- Accompanied by the original receipt or order number

## Exceptions (Non-Returnable Items)
The following categories are final sale and cannot be returned:
- Personal care items (opened headphones earbuds, cosmetics)
- Downloadable digital products and gift cards
- Items marked "Final Sale" at checkout
- Custom or personalised products

## Late Returns
Returns after 30 days are generally not accepted. In cases of defect or manufacturer fault within the warranty window (see Warranty Policy), a warranty claim is available instead of a standard return.

## How to Start a Return
1. Sign in to your ShopMax account and open the order.
2. Click "Return or Replace".
3. Choose a reason and print the pre-paid shipping label.
4. Drop the package at any approved carrier location.
